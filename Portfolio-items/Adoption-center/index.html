Adoption Fee

  • Puppy (under 1 year).................$600.00
  • Adult (1 -9 years)........................$550.00
  • Senior (ten years and up)........... $325.00

The adoption fee also includes vaccinations, de-worming, I.D. tag and microchip insertion. We gratefully accept any amount above the adoption fee. It is used as a donation to support our mission to save as many dogs as possible.
